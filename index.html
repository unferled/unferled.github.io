About

Unferled has helped dozens of high growth SaaS businesses achieve their ambitions and has been trusted by hundreds of founders and executives to help them achieve step change improvements in the impact they deliver.

Unferled specialises in working with startups and scaleups to maximise the leverage they have from their product and engineering functions. This is done by either working directly with the teams involved or as an advisor at the executive level.

Fergus founded Unferled to empower organisations with the foundations and the tools for lasting success and the vast majority of companies we work with go on to have successful exits, raise subsequent investment and/or exceed revenue targets either during or subsequent to us working together. Fergus is trusted by Founders, Technology Leaders and Investors to advise on key decisions and to be a fair and empathetic thought partner.

Fergus teaches and coaches technology executives as part of the CTO Programme at the University of Cambridge.
